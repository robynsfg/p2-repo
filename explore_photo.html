Adventurer: Brad Romano

Washington Trek

From Mt. Rainier to Mt. Olympus, Brad Romano embarked on a trek that would bring him through sleet and snow, but finished with one month's worth of incredible stories to share. What began as a solo trip to take a break from his position as a high school biology teacher turned into a friendly expedition as he met fellow hikers along his way. With his hammock in tow, Brad found his rhythm within the mountains, always making sure there were opportunities for a hang between the trees.
