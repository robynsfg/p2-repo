Parachute Hammock

This lightweight hammock is ideal for moderate to extreme adventuring, with rip-free nylon that maxes out at 1.2 pounds and can easily be shrunk down to fit neatly in a stuff sack. Tangle-resistant support ropes have tree hugger hooks that allow you to quickly snap this hammock on and off trunks and branches, getting you ready to relax in no time.
