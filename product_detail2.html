Airborne Hammock

When going high in the sky you'll need to have a reliable hammock that lets you enjoy the view, mind at ease. The Airborne hammock's thick nylon and reinforced strapping does just that, with carabiner clips to safely keep you on your rope. Additionally, the tightly wound cocoon shape keeps you planted without constricting movement.
