About Us

Cocoon Co. has been Vancouver’s one-stop shop for hammocks since 2001, and has proudly been providing more countries around the globe with hanging travel buddies ever since. Anyone can enjoy the comforts of a trusty hammock, and we believe that a hammock should be able to keep up with our adventurers’ lifestyles no matter where they go.
